Settings

Index

Settings


Your operator ID, name and other information are submitted with your counts.

They form a unique ID against the data to help check for errors, such as duplicate sends.

Once stored, this data will survive between reboots and applications closures. It will however be erased if this application is reinstalled or removed.

Journey Info

This information is entered at the start of your journey, and it is persistant until changed or you choose to end the journey after submitting a count.

Thge headcode and departure time are considered to be essential info.

However if you do not know the headcode (this will be known by the Train manager) the the departing station and time may be enough.

CRS

Index

Count Info

This application stores CRS data so you can lookup the three letter code of the stations.

These codes are standard throughout TOC's

The list rarely changes, but it is best to reload the data after every 2-4 weeks, or when you first use this application.

Simply press the update button and wait till the date on the page changes to todays date.

You can then go back to the count or journey page you were on, or just exit the application if you have no counts to submit.
