Journey Info

This information is entered at the start of your journey, and it is persistant until changed or you choose to end the journey after submitting a report.

The headcode or departing station & time are considered to be essential info.

However if you do not know the headcode (this will be known by the Train manager) please make sure to be as detailed with the journey information as possible.

This information will surivie between reboots and application closures as some people make the same journey many time.
